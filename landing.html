What We Do & Why We Do

The aim of Food Donation System is to collect the excess or leftover food from donors such as hotels, restaurants, marriage halls, etc. and distribute to the needy people.

Every day, a significant amount of edible food goes to waste, while countless individuals face the harsh reality of food insecurity.
